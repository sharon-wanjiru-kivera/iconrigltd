Our Comprehensive Water Solutions

ICON RIG LTD offers a full suite of services designed to meet all your water access and management needs. Our expertise ensures reliable, efficient, and sustainable water solutions for various applications.

  • Borehole Drilling

    We specialize in professional borehole drilling services, utilizing modern drilling rigs and techniques to access underground water sources efficiently and safely. Our process includes:

    • Site assessment and hydrogeological survey
    • Precision drilling based on geological data
    • Installation of casing and screen
    • Development and testing of the borehole

    We drill boreholes for domestic, agricultural, industrial, and municipal use.

  • Borehole Solarization

    Embrace clean and renewable energy for your water pumping needs. We design and install robust solar power systems specifically for boreholes, providing a cost-effective and environmentally friendly alternative to conventional power. Benefits include:

    • Reduced electricity costs
    • Reliable power in off-grid areas
    • Lower carbon footprint
    • Automated pumping based on sunlight

  • Borehole Maintenance

    Regular maintenance is vital for the longevity and performance of your borehole system. Our maintenance services help prevent issues and ensure a consistent water supply. Services include:

    • Routine pump inspection and servicing
    • Borehole cleaning and rehabilitation
    • Water quality testing and treatment recommendations
    • System efficiency checks

  • Pumps Installation

    Selecting and installing the right pump is crucial for optimal water extraction. We provide expert installation of various types of pumps, including submersible and surface pumps, tailored to your borehole's depth and water requirements. We ensure proper sizing, installation, and system integration.

  • Hydrogeological Survey

    Before drilling, a comprehensive hydrogeological survey is essential. Our experienced hydrogeologists conduct detailed surveys to:

    • Identify potential groundwater sources
    • Assess aquifer characteristics and sustainability
    • Determine optimal drilling locations and depths
    • Provide valuable data for borehole design

    This minimizes risks and maximizes the chances of a successful borehole.

  • Water Storage Facilities

    Ensure a continuous and reliable water supply with our water storage solutions. We design and construct various types of water storage facilities, including:

    • Water tanks (plastic, steel, concrete)
    • Reservoirs and storage ponds

    Our storage solutions are built for durability and sized to meet your specific needs, providing backup during dry periods or for increased demand.
