Founded with a clear vision to address the growing need for sustainable water access, ICON RIG LTD has established itself as a reputable and reliable provider of borehole drilling and comprehensive water management services. Our journey began with a commitment to leveraging expertise and technology to unlock vital water resources for various applications.

Our mission is to provide high-quality, sustainable, and reliable water solutions that empower communities, support businesses, and enhance agricultural productivity. We strive to be a leader in the industry by delivering exceptional service, promoting environmental stewardship, and fostering long-term relationships with our clients.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We conduct our business with honesty, transparency, and ethical practices.
  • Quality: We are committed to delivering the highest standards of quality in every project we undertake.
  • Innovation: We embrace new technologies and innovative approaches to provide efficient and effective water solutions.
  • Sustainability: We are dedicated to promoting sustainable water management practices for the benefit of future generations.
  • Client Satisfaction: We prioritize understanding and meeting the unique needs of our clients, ensuring their satisfaction.

Our Team

Our strength lies in our team of experienced hydrogeologists, drillers, engineers, and technicians. Their collective expertise and dedication are the driving force behind our success and our ability to tackle complex water challenges.

At ICON RIG LTD, we are more than just a service provider; we are your partner in securing essential water resources.
