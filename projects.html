Our Projects: Delivering Water Solutions Across Sectors

Here are some examples of our diverse projects, showcasing our capability to deliver effective water solutions for various clients and needs. These projects highlight our expertise in borehole drilling, solarization, maintenance, and water storage.

Community Water Access Project - Rural Village [Location Example]

Scope: Hydrogeological survey, borehole drilling, submersible pump installation, and construction of a 10,000-liter elevated water tank.

Details: This project aimed to provide clean and accessible water to a remote rural community. We conducted a thorough survey to identify a sustainable aquifer, drilled a successful borehole, installed a reliable pump, and built a water storage facility. The project significantly improved the community's health and reduced the time spent fetching water. Challenges included difficult terrain, which was overcome by utilizing specialized drilling equipment.

Agricultural Irrigation Enhancement - Large Scale Farm [Location Example]

Scope: Drilling of multiple boreholes, installation of high-capacity solar-powered pumps, and integration with existing irrigation network.

Details: To enhance irrigation for a large agricultural farm, we drilled several boreholes to increase water availability. A key component was the installation of solar-powered pumping systems, drastically reducing the farm's electricity costs and environmental impact. This led to improved crop yields and operational efficiency. The project involved careful planning to integrate the new water sources with the farm's existing infrastructure.

Scope: Residential borehole drilling, installation of a submersible pump with a solar power system, and a small water storage tank.
